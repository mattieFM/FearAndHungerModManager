Boss Rush of the Endless

Boss Rush of the Endless is a standalone challenge mode that drops you into an unbroken gauntlet of boss fights. The moment one boss falls, the next is summoned. A kill counter tracks your streak. How far can you go before your candle goes out?

How to Start

  1. From the main title screen select Boss Rush (only visible when the mod is enabled).
  2. Choose your starting character if prompted.
  3. The first boss encounter begins immediately.

Boss Rush uses a separate game state from your main save files. Starting a Boss Rush run does not overwrite or affect your regular saves.

Boss Pool

The Boss Rush mode draws from the full pool of Fear & Hunger bosses, including:

  • Grogoroth, the Dungeon Lord
  • Sylvian, God of the Sun
  • Gohaf the Crow Mauler
  • Ragnvaldr the Knight
  • Cahara the Mercenary
  • Enki the Philosopher
  • Moonless
  • Other high-tier enemies classified as boss troops

Bosses are drawn in a randomised order. No two runs are guaranteed to present them in the same sequence.

Progressive Scaling

As your kill count rises, progressive scaling applies stat multipliers to each subsequent boss:

  • Rounds 1–5: Standard boss stats.
  • Rounds 6–10: +25% HP and ATK.
  • Rounds 11–20: +50% HP and ATK.
  • Round 21+: +100% HP and ATK (doubled).

The scaling stacks with Multiplayer player-count scaling and any Difficulty Mods you have enabled.
